Kung Pao Chicken

A classic Sichuan dish featuring stir-fried chicken with peanuts and a savory, spicy sauce.

Ingredients:

2 boneless chicken breasts (diced)
1/2 cup peanuts
2 cloves garlic (minced)
1-inch ginger (minced)
3 dried red chilies (cut into halves)
1/2 red bell pepper (diced)
1/2 green bell pepper (diced)
2 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp oyster sauce
1 tbsp rice vinegar
1 tbsp hoisin sauce
1 tsp cornstarch (mixed with 2 tbsp water)
1 tbsp sesame oil
1 tbsp cooking oil
1/2 tsp sugar
Green onions for garnish

Steps:

1. Heat oil in a wok, stir-fry dried red chilies until aromatic.
2. Add garlic, ginger, and chicken; stir-fry until the chicken turns golden.
3. Add bell peppers and peanuts; stir for a minute.
4. Pour in soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, vinegar, sugar, and cornstarch slurry.
5. Stir well until the sauce thickens and coats the chicken.
6. Garnish with green onions and serve with steamed rice.

Sweet and Sour Pork

A delicious Cantonese dish with crispy pork coated in a tangy sauce.

Ingredients:

250g pork (cut into cubes)
2 tbsp cornstarch
1 egg (beaten)
1/2 cup flour
1/2 cup bell peppers (diced)
1/4 cup pineapple chunks
2 tbsp tomato ketchup
1 tbsp vinegar
1 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp sugar
1/2 tsp salt
2 tbsp water
Oil for frying

Steps:

1. Coat pork cubes with egg, flour, and cornstarch.
2. Deep fry until golden brown and set aside.
3. In a pan, heat 1 tbsp oil and stir-fry bell peppers and pineapple.
4. dd ketchup, vinegar, soy sauce, sugar, salt, and water. Stir well.
5. Toss in fried pork and mix until well coated.
6. Serve hot with rice.

Egg Fried Rice

A quick and easy Chinese staple packed with flavor.

Ingredients:

2 cups cooked rice (preferably day-old)
2 eggs (beaten)
1/2 cup mixed vegetables (peas, carrots, corn)
2 tbsp soy sauce
1 tsp sesame oil
1/2 tsp white pepper
2 cloves garlic (minced)
1 green onion (chopped)
1 tbsp cooking oil

Steps:

1. Heat oil in a pan and scramble the eggs. Set aside.
2. In the same pan, stir-fry garlic and mixed vegetables for 2 minutes.
3. Add cooked rice and stir-fry for another 2 minutes.
4. Pour in soy sauce, sesame oil, and white pepper. Mix well.
5. Add scrambled eggs back and stir to combine.
6. Garnish with green onions and serve hot.

Hot and Sour Soup

A comforting and flavorful Chinese soup with a perfect balance of spice and tang.

Ingredients:

4 cups chicken broth
1/2 cup mushrooms (sliced)
1/2 cup tofu (cubed)
1/4 cup bamboo shoots (optional)
2 tbsp soy sauce
1 tbsp rice vinegar
1/2 tsp black pepper
1/2 tsp white pepper
1 tbsp cornstarch (mixed with 2 tbsp water)
1 egg (beaten)
1 tsp sesame oil
2 green onions (chopped)

Steps:

1. Bring chicken broth to a boil in a pot.
2. Add mushrooms, tofu, and bamboo shoots. Let it simmer for 5 minutes.
3. Stir in soy sauce, vinegar, black pepper, and white pepper.
4. Slowly pour in the cornstarch slurry, stirring continuously.
5. Slowly drizzle in beaten egg while stirring to create egg ribbons.
6. Finish with sesame oil and garnish with green onions. Serve hot!
